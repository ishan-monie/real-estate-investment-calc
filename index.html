Market Insights & Tips

Property Research Tips

  • Always verify ARV with recent comparable sales
  • Factor in holding costs and unexpected repairs
  • Consider neighborhood trends and future development
  • Check for liens, taxes, and legal issues

Risk Factors

  • Market volatility can affect ARV estimates
  • Construction costs may exceed initial estimates
  • Time on market can impact profitability
  • Interest rate changes affect financing costs

Formula Breakdown

Max Offer Price = ARV - Transaction Costs - Investor ROI - Rehab Costs - Wholesale Fee

Estimated Profit = Max Offer Price - Actual Purchase Price

Transaction Costs = ARV × Transaction %

Investor ROI = ARV × ROI %

Rehab Costs = Square Feet × Rehab per Sq Ft
